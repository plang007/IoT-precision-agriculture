About IoTPA

Taking the advantage of the decreasing price of sensors, we aim to provide an IoT solution to the sensing technology for precision agriculture. The core of our system is the Wireless Sensor Network (WSN) which consists of a variety of on-site sensors to acquire useful data of the crop farms.

Compared with remote sensing technology like satellite and drones, our solution has advantages in several aspects:

Stability

Unlike the drone and satellite solutions which are greatly affected by the weather conditions, our solution is very stable.

Scalability

Unlike the drone solution, which requires much more operating labors, the sensor network is very easy to scale up with just utilizing more sensor nodes.

Accessibility

IoT solution can provide real-time data 24 hours a day, whereas the drone and satellite may take a day or up to a week to revisit the entire farm to get the data.

A potential solution to food crisis


The main concept of precision agriculture is to increase the accuracy and controllability of the farming practice. By gaining the real-time feedback, the inputs to the farming could be reduced without affecting the yield so that the production efficiency can be greatly increased.

It could potentially alleviate the food crisis brought by the increasing world population, which is estimated to reach 9.15 billion by 2050. The market of precision agriculture keeps growing in recent years, which now sees an annual revenue of $1.8 billion with a growth rate of 12.8%.
